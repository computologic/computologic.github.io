Marketec for PC (Windows Store Version)

Marketec for PC is designed as a business tool for offline and cloud-synced data management. To support our licensing and core features, we handle data as follows:

  • Personal Information: We collect Company Name (required), and optionally User Name and Email Address. This data is used solely for license registration, 45-day trial authentication, and providing technical support.
  • Location Data: The Marketec PC application does not access, collect, or transmit your device's geographical location.
  • Business Data: Your invoices, stock, and sales data are stored locally on your device. Full license users may choose to store information online for cloud synchronization and license verification.
  • Support Backups: You may voluntarily send database backups to our server for support; we do not request or automatically collect backups.

No other personal data is collected, tracked, or shared with third parties.

Marketec Lookup (Android App)

The Marketec Android app collects the following data to provide its core business features:

  • Location: We collect Approximate and Precise Location data to enable salesman check-in verification and route tracking. This data is only collected while the app is in use (Foreground) and is optional.
  • User IDs: We collect Account IDs and Email addresses for the purpose of account management and license authentication.
  • Device Identifiers: We may collect device IDs for app functionality and security.

Data Security: All data is encrypted in transit using secure protocols. We do not share your location or personal information with third-party advertisers or external services.
