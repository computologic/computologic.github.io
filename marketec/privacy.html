At Computologic, we prioritize the privacy of our business users. This policy outlines how we handle data within the Marketec software ecosystem.

1. Marketec for PC (Windows Store Version)

Marketec for PC is a business tool for offline and cloud-synced data management. Data handling is as follows:

  • Personal Information: We collect Company Name (required), and optionally User Name and Email Address for license registration, trial authentication, and technical support.
  • Location Data: Marketec for PC does not access, collect, or transmit your device's geographical location.
  • Business Data: Your invoices, stock, and sales data are stored locally. Full license users may use secure servers for cloud synchronization.
  • Support Backups: Database backups are only handled if you voluntarily send them to us for support purposes.

2. Marketec Lookup (Android App)

The Marketec Android app is a mobile companion for businesses. Data handling is as follows:

  • Location Data: This application does not access or transmit your device's location.
  • User Identifiers: Only Client Key, User ID, and Password are collected to connect to the Firebase server for authentication and data access. No email addresses or other personal information are collected.
  • Device Identifiers: No device IDs are collected.

3. Data Security and Retention

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS).
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or business data with third parties.
  • Retention: We retain license and registration information for the duration of your active license. You may request data deletion at any time.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about your account or request its deletion. For privacy-related inquiries or data removal requests, please contact our support team.
