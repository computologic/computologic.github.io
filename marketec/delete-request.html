Marketec Data Deletion Portal

This portal allows users of the Marketec Lookup mobile application to request the permanent deletion of their account and associated personal data in accordance with Google Play's User Data Policy.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Provide your Client Key and Username in the form below.
  2. Click Submit Deletion Request to email info@computologic.com.
  3. The team at Computologic will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days.

Data Removal & Retention

What is deleted: We store minimal user data. Upon a verified request, Computologic will permanently remove:

  • Login Credentials: Your Username and Password will be completely purged from our Firestore database.
  • User Identifiers: Your unique User ID and account profile.
  • Location Data: Any approximate or precise location logs synced to the server.

Retention Period: All requested data is permanently scrubbed within 30 days of request receipt. Once the account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
